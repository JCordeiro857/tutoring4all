02 — The Problem

What's Holding Your Website Back

A website audit of tutoring4all.com.au revealed several issues that are likely reducing visitor trust and discouraging enquiries.

📱

Poor Mobile Experience

Over 70% of your visitors are on phones. The current site isn't optimised for mobile, leading to frustrated visitors who bounce before enquiring.

👀

Outdated Visual Design

The design doesn't match the professionalism of your service. First impressions matter — parents are judging your credibility from the homepage in seconds.

🔍

No Clear Call-to-Action

Visitors aren't guided clearly toward booking or enquiring. A confused visitor is a lost customer — the site needs stronger conversion paths.

⚡

Slow Load Times

Unoptimised assets make the site feel sluggish. Google also penalises slow sites in search rankings, reducing your organic visibility.

🏆

No Trust Signals

The WWCC certification, 10+ years experience, and NSW curriculum alignment — these trust signals aren't visible or prominent enough on the current site.

🎨

Inconsistent Branding

No strong visual identity makes the business appear less established than it is. A consistent colour palette and typography builds instant brand recognition.
