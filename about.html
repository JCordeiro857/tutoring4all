Our Story

Founded on a Simple Belief

Tutoring4All was founded by Cinzia Favotto in 2013 after recognising that students needed more than just academic assistance — they required guidance from experienced mentors who genuinely cared about their growth.

What began as a small tutoring service in Sydney's Inner West has grown into a trusted team of qualified educators, supporting students from Kindergarten through to Year 12 HSC across Sydney's Inner West and southern regions.

Over a decade later, our mission remains exactly the same: to provide every student with personalised, compassionate, and curriculum-aligned support that builds both their knowledge and their confidence.
