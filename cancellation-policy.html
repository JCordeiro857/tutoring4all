Cancellation Policy

Our cancellation policy is built on mutual respect — protecting both tutors and families fairly.

💡 Our core principle: We recognise that life happens — emergencies arise on both sides. Our policy is designed to protect tutors' preparation time while remaining fair and reasonable for families.

⏰

Student Cancellation — Less than 6 Hours Notice

50%

A fee of 50% of the session cost applies. The tutor receives 50% of their rate for the time they have set aside and prepared for.

🚪

No Notice — Tutor Arrives to Empty Home

75%

A fee of 75% of the session cost applies. The tutor receives 75% of their rate to account for travel time and full session preparation.

📅

Tutor Cancellation — Less than 6 Hours Notice

5% off

A 5% discount is applied to the lesson cost. We hold our tutors to the same standard of reliability we expect from families.

Full Policy Details

1. Purpose

This cancellation policy exists to establish a fair and transparent framework that respects the time, preparation, and commitment of both tutors and families. Tutoring4All is committed to ensuring both parties are treated with mutual respect and professionalism.

2. Student / Parent Cancellations

Less than 6 hours' notice: Where a student or parent cancels a session with less than 6 hours' notice, a cancellation fee of 50% of the session rate will apply. The tutor will be compensated at 50% of their agreed rate.

No notice given (tutor arrives to an empty home): In circumstances where no notice is given and the tutor arrives at the agreed location to find no one present, a fee of 75% of the session rate applies. The tutor will be compensated at 75% of their agreed rate to account for travel time and full preparation.

3. Tutor Cancellations

Where a tutor cancels a session with less than 6 hours' notice, a 5% discount will be applied to the lesson cost as a goodwill gesture to the family. Tutoring4All will make every effort to arrange a replacement tutor or reschedule the session within the same week.

4. Rescheduling

Where feasible, Tutoring4All will endeavour to reschedule any cancelled sessions within the same week for both parties. Rescheduling is always preferable to cancellation where possible, and we encourage families and tutors to communicate as early as possible when plans change.

5. Emergencies

We understand that genuine emergencies arise. Where a family or tutor needs to cancel due to a medical emergency, illness, or other serious unforeseen circumstance, we encourage open communication with our team and will consider each situation with empathy and flexibility.

6. Contact

To cancel or reschedule a session, please contact us as early as possible at 0490 423 713 or info@Tutoring4All.com.au.
